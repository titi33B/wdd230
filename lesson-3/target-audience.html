Target Audience

The target audience is community members intersted in getting involved in local event and those who wants to support small busineses. It is also to target those who are seeking to start their own businesses locally and want to be supported along the way.

Personas

Scenarios

  1. I want to help our businesses grow
  2. I want to find local businesses and information about them
  3. I want to know more about this town
  4. A gathering place for business owners and people interested in helping local businesses
  5. Contact information for local business owners and chamber of commerce members
